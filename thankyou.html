About our Website

Our Website help users discover and connect with local businesses,
service providers, farmers, vendors, and home-based entrepreneurs in their area.
The platform should allow businesses and freelancers to register their services,
showcase their products, and receive customer inquiries.
Users should be able to search, filter, and
review businesses and services based on location,
category, service and ratings.
